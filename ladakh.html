LADAKH
"Ladakh is not just a destination, it's a journey through the heavens on Earth."

Ladakh, often referred to as "the Land of High Passes," is a stunning region in northern India, nestled between the majestic Himalayas and the Karakoram Range. Known for its dramatic landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, and rich Tibetan Buddhist culture, Ladakh offers a paradise for adventure seekers, trekkers, and those looking for tranquility. Key attractions include Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, and the historic monasteries of Hemis and Thiksey. The unique high-altitude desert scenery and vibrant festivals make Ladakh a must-visit destination for nature and culture lovers alike.

  • Special Food: Thukpa, Momos
  • Best Time: May to September
  • Solo Travel Expense: ₹10,000-₹15,000 (4-5 days)
  • Family Travel Expense: ₹25,000-₹50,000
