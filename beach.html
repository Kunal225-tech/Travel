Top Beaches in India

Varkala Beach (Kerala)

This cliff-lined beach in Kerala is known for its natural springs and holistic healing centers. Varkala Beach offers a more peaceful alternative to the crowded beaches of Goa, with breathtaking views and spiritual significance, often referred to as Papanasam Beach for its religious value.

Known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life, Tarkarli Beach is a haven for water sports enthusiasts. Scuba diving, snorkeling, and boat rides to spot dolphins are popular activities here. The nearby Sindhudurg Fort adds a historical flavor to the area.
